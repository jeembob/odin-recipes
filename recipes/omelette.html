French Omelette

Ingredients

  • (2) eggs
  • Olive oil
  • water
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Crack eggs in easily pourable vessel and beat until consistent yellow/orange
  2. Heat layer of oil on medium-high heat
  3. Pour eggs and stir while maintaining a layer of egg in pan at all times
  4. Roll eggs
  5. Add salt to taste
